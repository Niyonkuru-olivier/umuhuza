About Us

Our Mission: UMUHUZA is a revolutionary web and mobile application that bridges the gap in Rwanda's food chain. We empower farmers with vital information, connect them to essential players like agro-dealers and processors, and create a more efficient and transparent market system.

Why we exist: Rwanda's agricultural sector is fragmented, leading to information asymmetry and unreliable access to markets. Farmers struggle to get fair prices, while consumers and businesses face challenges finding consistent, high-quality produce.

The good news: UMUHUZA's user-friendly web and mobile app provides farmers with:

  1. Information access: Best practices, weather forecasts, and market data.
  2. Market access: Connect directly with agro-dealers, processors, and consumers.
  3. Improved pricing: Gain market transparency and fairer deals for their crops.
