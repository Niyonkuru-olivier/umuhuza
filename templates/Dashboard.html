Factors Affecting Crop Yields

The yields of maize and beans have been influenced by several factors over the years:

  • Weather: Rainfall and temperature changes have greatly affected the growth cycles of crops.
  • Fertilizers: The use of fertilizers like NPK, DAP, and organic composts has enhanced productivity, but availability and affordability remain challenges.
  • Government Policy: Support from the Rwandan government through subsidies, extension services, and irrigation projects has contributed positively to yield increases.
