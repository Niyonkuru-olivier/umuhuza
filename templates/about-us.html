About Us

Bridging Farmers with the World of Agriculture

Our Purpose

Mission: Our mission is to be the bridge between farmers and all other parties involved in the agriculture sector. We aim to provide farmers with access to information, resources, and support to improve productivity, sustainability, and overall success.

Vision: UMUHUZA envisions a future where collaborative intelligence is at the heart of agriculture. By creating a platform that connects farmers, agro-dealers, researchers, processors, investors, customers, and policymakers, we strive to foster innovation and ensure sustainable agricultural growth.

Goals: Our platform aims to achieve several key objectives:

  1. 1. To facilitate knowledge sharing and collaboration across the agricultural sector
  2. 2. To provide access to critical resources and tools for farmers to enhance their operations
  3. 3. To foster connections between all stakeholders in agriculture, from farmers to investors
  4. 4. To promote sustainable agricultural practices through data-driven insights and innovation

Organization & Project Background

UMUHUZA began its operations in 2025, with the vision of transforming the agricultural landscape in Rwanda and beyond.

Founded by Mr Olivier Niyonkuru and Ashula Ishimwe, a visionary in the agriculture sector, UMUHUZA has been driven by the belief that connecting stakeholders

through innovative technology can revolutionize farming and agricultural practices.

Objectives of the Agricultural Platform

The UMUHUZA platform has been designed to address the specific needs of the agricultural community in Rwanda and globally. Our objectives include providing a

centralized platform where farmers can access critical information such as market trends, weather updates, and best practices. We aim to empower farmers with the

latest agricultural research and development, ensuring they can make informed decisions about their crops, livestock, and overall farming techniques.Another key

objective is to bridge the gap between farmers and investors. By providing a platform where investors can easily access information about farming opportunities and

challenges, we hope to drive more investment into the sector, improving overall productivityand sustainability. UMUHUZA also aims to foster collaboration between

different stakeholders in agriculture, from researchers to agro-dealers. By connecting these groups,we can facilitate the exchange of ideas and solutions, driving

innovation and addressing the challenges faced by the agriculture sector in Rwanda.

The Importance of UMUHUZA for Agricultural Development in Rwanda

As a predominantly agricultural country, Rwanda stands to benefit greatly from innovations in farming practices. UMUHUZA plays a vital role in

transforming the way agriculture operates by providing farmers with access to real-time information, connecting them to markets, and offering

insights into best practices.

Through UMUHUZA, we aim to boost productivity, enhance sustainability, and promote rural development. This platform is an essential tool in the

modernization of Rwanda's agricultural sector, contributing to food security, poverty alleviation, and overall economic growth.
